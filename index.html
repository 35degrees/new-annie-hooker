Mixed Media

I’m most interested in education through interactive digital media. A year into my web development venture, I’m exploring coding languages and digital libraries to see what methods work best with how I learn.

Using my MBA and CFP experience, my main interactive project revolve around learning about personal finance and business trends. I’m also passionate about politics, sports and art, and I use these topics in my apps to push my web development skills. I’m also interest in early education and plan to develop an app for K-8 students using interactive learning.

I was instantly attracted to the D3js library, which is a framework I use to create my data visualizations and graphs. Combined with the interactivity of the Javascript language, I see a powerful platform to engage people visually and stimulate their desire to learn more.

From San Francisco originally, I am based in Reno, Nevada, where I can launch to Tahoe, Yosemite and other outdoor spots, and with my CFP in hand, I deploy my skills on a custom t-shirt sales team.
